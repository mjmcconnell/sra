Welcome to sharon regan art

I often find my inspiration whilst out walking the dogs or exploring country lanes on horseback, I love to catch sight of hares, foxes and other wildlife or watching the horses play in the field, it feels like a wonderful gift, a special moment. I aim to capture that moment and bring the outdoors indoors. Watching my subjects gives me pleasure, creating the sculptures from clay brings me pleasure, I hope my sculptures bring you pleasure too.

Gallery

My sculptures reflect my fascination with the duality of strength and fragility found within human existence and nature.

Questions? Comments? Let's talk.

Fell free to drop me a message for any enquiries about my work, for just for a chat.
